Jaadu LED Lighting Solutions is a privately owned and operated Australian company leading the way in sustainable project solutions, and is one of the fastest growing lighting solutions providers in Australia. Jaadu is steadfast about providing superior quality products, matched to our customer needs as part of Victorian government schemes to increase energy efficiency and helping to create a more sustainable Australia.

Our Partnership

Jaadu Lighting Solutions works in partnership with multiple Approved Persons (APs) under the Victorian Energy Upgrades program, such as EcoCare Carbon Solutions and LED Saves, in order to help our customers improve the energy efficiency of their homes and businesses.
